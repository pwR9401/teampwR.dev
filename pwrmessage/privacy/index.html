pwRMessage Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes the policies and procedures of pwRMessage (hereinafter referred to as "the Service," "we," "us," or "our") regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

The Service is designed with a minimal-data collection philosophy. We collect and store your chosen username in a plaintext format to facilitate user discovery and communication routing. We do not store your actual password; instead, we store a cryptographic hash of your password to ensure that your actual credentials remain protected and are never known to us. Furthermore, pwRMessage does not log Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, and we do not maintain records of the specific network locations or hardware identifiers from which you access the Service.

Regarding communication data, the Service does not store your messages server-side. Messages are transmitted between users and reside only on the local storage of the sending and receiving devices. Once a message is successfully delivered, it does not reside on our infrastructure. We do not log, monitor, or inspect any data passing through our servers during the transmission process. Our infrastructure acts solely as a conduit for delivery without surveillance of the content or metadata of your communications.

By using the Service, you acknowledge and accept specific conditions regarding your visibility and presence. The Service automatically tracks whether you are currently connected to our servers. When another user initiates a chat with you, the Service will display your current status as online or offline. Additionally, you acknowledge that the current technical architecture of the Service allows users with sufficient technical knowledge to access and view the entire list of all currently online users. If you require absolute privacy regarding your connection status, you should take this into account before using the Service.

We reserve the right, at our sole discretion and without prior notice, to suspend or permanently ban your account for violations of our terms or to maintain the integrity of the Service. Furthermore, users may request the permanent deletion of their account or a manual password reset by contacting us at the support address provided below. Please note that while we can facilitate a password change upon verified request, our administrators cannot retrieve your original password due to our cryptographic hashing security measures.

We retain your username and password hash for as long as your account remains active. We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Every time the Privacy Policy is updated, you will receive an in-app notification regarding the changes, and the effective date at the top of this document will be updated accordingly. The most current version of this policy will always be available at https://teampwr.dev/pwrmessage/privacy. If you have any questions regarding this policy or our data practices, you may contact us at pwrmessage@support.teampwr.dev.
